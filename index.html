About Me

Based out of Calgary, Alberta, Canada, I am currently attending Dominican University of California remotely in the ACS program, while also working as a Dev Degree Intern at Shopify. I used to be a Registered Nurse and did most of my work with a not-for-profit that provides comprehensive health and social services to people experiencing homelessness in Calgary. Prior to my nursing career I studied some Engineering at the University of Waterloo and completed a Bachelor's of Science in Biology at the University of Calgary.
